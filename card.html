Northern Redbreast Tilapia

This specie is a substrate spawner, with fishes forming pairs and exhibiting biparental guarding behaviour.

  • Scientific Name:Tilapia Zillii
  • Average Length:40cm
  • Average Lifespan:7 years
  • Habitat:Freshwater lakes and mashes

Just like all other members of the genus Cotodon their breast and belly are often tinted red. Head and rump are olive-green on their top and paler on their sides. They grow to a maximum length of 45 centimeters and maximum weight of 2.5 kilograms.
